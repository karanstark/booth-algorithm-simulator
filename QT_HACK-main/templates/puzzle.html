Standard Operating Procedure

01

Objective

Build a quantum circuit implementing Booth's multiplication algorithm for two 2-bit numbers (0–3). The product must appear in P, inputs must be preserved.

Procedure

1
Choose Inputs

Select A and B from the left panel.

2
Initialize

X gates in column 1.

3
Booth Encode

Compute e0, e1 from B.

4
Process d0 = -b0

Subtract A at position 0.

5
Process d1 = b0-b1

Add/subtract A at position 1.

6
Process d2 = b1

Add A at position 2.

7
Verify

Check Circuit.

07

Pro Tips

All clicks for multi-qubit gates must be in the same column.
Booth subtraction = negate A + add + 1 (two's complement).
Always uncompute carries after each phase.
